Website Plan

Site Name

Onix Performance Hub

This name represents a website focused on car customization, maintenance, and performance improvements, especially for Chevrolet Onix owners.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about car maintenance, performance upgrades, parts tracking, and customization tips. It helps car owners manage and improve their vehicles.

Scenarios

  • What upgrades can improve my car performance?
  • How can I track maintenance and replacement parts?

Color Scheme

Blue (#0077cc) – used for buttons, links, and highlights

Dark Gray (#222) – used for headers, text, and backgrounds

Typography

Arial – used for general content

Roboto – used for headings and emphasis
