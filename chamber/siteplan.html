João Pessoa Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

João Pessoa Chamber of Commerce. This website will represent the local chamber of commerce and help promote business growth and economic opportunities in João Pessoa, Brazil.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about local businesses, events, and economic development in João Pessoa. The site will help connect business owners, investors, and the community.

Target Audience

  • Local business owners
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Investors
  • Residents of João Pessoa
  • Visitors looking for local businesses and services

Scenarios

  • A business owner wants to learn how to join the Chamber of Commerce.
  • A resident is looking for upcoming community or business events.
  • A visitor wants to discover businesses and services available in João Pessoa.
